By Daniel J Noh

I hope that your experience navigating this website is as delightful as creating it was for me. This website/visual essay was created for a course taught by Elan Ullendorf, Escape the Algorithm. And sorry if you're on mobile. I didn't have the cognitive capacity to make it responsive.

Dedication

It has been quite the semester for me, for reasons I won’t profess on a class assignment. My friends, however, have been incredible. I have had such a newfound respect and love for some of my closest friends, so I dedicate this project to them.

Design & Development

This website was designed and developed, with delight and care, in HTML & CSS with a sprinkle of JavaScript/jQuery. It is hosted on Github and you can judge my web development abilities here. The assets were all hand(-digitally) drawn on Adobe PSD. The fonts are Poppins & Frank Ruhl Libre, both available on Google Fonts.
